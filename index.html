CultureShock! Bahrain (Culture Shock!) Paperback

The social climate in Bahrain is relatively liberalized while Islamic teachings are strict. This temperate environment has allowed space for business growth and trade. Discover the rich Bahraini dress customs and the importance of ubiquitous mosques in this little sheikhdom area. In Surprise of Culture! Bahrain, gain insights into the business etiquette and gather tips on socializing and fitting in—all key to home-building and working in this fast-growing Middle East society.
